About us

Welcome to Inferno gym


In a total area of more than 1000 m 2 everyone will find something for themselves - a gym with a cardio program or group activities (yoga, pilates, core, cardio). After training, you can relax with a massage by a physiotherapist. We also provide the services of our nutrition team.
Come and see why people choose us!


Functionality


The functionality of the club is reflected in the equipment of the space itself, the coaches and the service provided. In accordance with this, out fitness studios have a modernly designed interior, ventilation, air conditioning, reception area, sports supplements, locker rooms with lockers, showers, aerobics room, professional Technogym Cardio and Strenght equipment and extremely acceptable membership fees.

Equipment


Over 60 Cardio Technogym machines and over 60 Technogym load machines and 25 benches and exercise equipment make this space complete and exercise productive. In addition, we have 3 large halls for group training.

Professionalism


The Faculty of Sports and Physical Education and several high schools for sports coaches form the base for instructors and personal trainers. In order to follow world trends when it comes to fitness, we try to gather as much information as possible through conventions, the Internet, magazines, professional literature and from our colleagues from countries where fitness is extremely developed.
