Training starts at full hour and lasts 1.5 hours to avoid crowds between groups in the locker rooms.
Please schedule the training you plan to attend, as well as not be late for it. In case you have scheduled a training and you have changed your mind - cancel it.
Equipment-sneakers and a towel are required for training.

*NOTE: You can schedule the training no later than one hour before the start, and cancel it no later than half an hour before the start.
